About Us

If you are a man who has ever shopped for lingerie for a woman, I am sure you can attest that it can be a very difficult process. Not only is it awkward to enter a lingerie store in the first place, it is extremely difficult to navigate through the alarming number of sizing and style options.

The TBar.com was designed with these difficulties in mind. We believe shopping for lingerie should be a fun and hassle free experience, without ever having to worry about the sizing conundrum or weeding through and overwhelming number of choices.

Our Roots

The T*Bar was birthed from a successful lingerie retail store located in Boulder, CO. The store was created with the strong, independent and self confident women in mind, while also making men feel at home while shopping for their partner.
